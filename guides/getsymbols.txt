How to get stock symbols for various stock exchanges

NSE INDIA
=========

1. Download the latest Bhavcopy from NSE website
http://www.nseindia.com/content/equities/cmbhav.htm
2. Cell O2 : Apply formula =(TRIM(LEFT(A2,9)) & ".NS")
3. Click the cell O2 and drag the fill handle down to the bottom

BSE INDIA
=========

1. Go to BSE Market Watch
	http://www.bseindia.com/markets/equity/EQReports/MarketWatch.aspx?expandable=2
2. Download excel file
3. Cell L2 : Apply formula =(TRIM(LEFT(B2,9)) & ".BO")
4. Click the cell L2 and drag the fill handle down to the bottom
